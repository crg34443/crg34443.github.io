About Us

Jacob and Emily’s love story began in San Francisco, where a shared passion for coffee brought them together. What started as casual conversations over cappuccinos soon became a dream to create a place that celebrated the warmth, flavor, and connection found in every cup. Their journey eventually led them to Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, a city whose charm inspired them to bring that dream to life. With care and dedication, they opened Brews & Bonds, a cozy café designed to capture both the spirit of their story and the vibrant coffee culture they fell in love with. Today, Brews & Bonds is more than a coffee shop. It’s a welcoming space where friends meet, stories are shared, and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the air. For Jacob and Emily, every cup served is a reminder of where their journey began — and the community that continues to grow around it.
